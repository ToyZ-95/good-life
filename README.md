# 🌿 Good Life
**Small steps, big changes.**
Good Life is a powerful, privacy-focused habit tracker designed to help you build consistency and achieve your goals. With beautiful heatmap charts, streak tracking, and interactive widgets, staying on track has never been easier.

[![Platform](https://img.shields.io/badge/platform-Android-brightgreen.svg)](https://github.com/ToyZ-95/good-life/releases/latest)
---
## ✨ Key Features
- **📊 Visual Progress**: Track your consistency with GitHub-style heatmap charts and detailed streak statistics.
- **📱 Interactive Widgets**: Complete your habits and manage your To-Do list directly from your home screen without opening the app.
- **⏲️ Timer-Based Habits**: Stay focused on time-boxed activities with built-in timers.
- **🔔 Smart Reminders**: Never miss a beat with customizable notifications and alarms.
- **📝 Integrated Todo List**: A dedicated space for one-off tasks right alongside your habits.
- **🚀 Auto-Updater**: The app notifies you and helps you install the latest version directly from GitHub.

## 📥 How to Install
1.  **Download the APK**: Click the **[Latest Release](https://github.com/ToyZ-95/good-life/releases/latest)** button above or go to the [Releases](https://github.com/ToyZ-95/good-life/releases) page.
2.  **Open the File**: Once downloaded, tap the APK file.
3.  **Enable Unknown Sources**: If prompted, allow your browser or file manager to "Install unknown apps."
4.  **Launch & Track**: Open **Good Life** and start building your better self!
